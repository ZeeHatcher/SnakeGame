You are a snake with one life.

Collect food Ïƒ to grow longer and earn a higher score.
Golden food are worth twice as much, but also increases your speed.

Every 3 points will make the game harder by incrementing the number of obstacles
and increasing the snake speed.

Press the arrow keys to move up, down, left and right.
Press escape to pause the game.
